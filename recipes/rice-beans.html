Best Rice Beans Recipe in Kenya

This is a very common dish esp among young people. It is easy to make and affordable


Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 1 Cup Beans
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 2 Tomatoes

Process

  1. In boiling water add the Rice and mix until it is firm
  2. In a pan with hot oil add your chopped onion
  3. Once the onion is browned, add your tomatoes
  4. Add your beans to the tomatoes and mix till they all come together
  5. Serve and plate while hot.
